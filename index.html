About this pilot.

  1. The assistant is trained exclusively on the GovernRight v7 user manual.
  2. Responses are generated by an AI model. The assistant declines questions outside the manual.
  3. Content updates are managed through a dashboard, no developer required.
  4. This demo runs on the free tier and may have usage limits during testing.
